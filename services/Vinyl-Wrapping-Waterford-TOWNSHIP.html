What is vinyl wrapping?

Vinyl wrapping is a process of applying a vinyl film to your vehicle to change its appearance.

Can I choose any color for vinyl wrapping?

Yes, there are countless colors and finishes available for vinyl wraps.

How long does vinyl wrapping last?

Vinyl wraps can last between 5 to 7 years with proper care.

Can vinyl wraps protect my original paint?

Yes, they can protect the underlying paint from scratches and UV damage.

Is vinyl wrapping reversible?

Yes, vinyl wraps can be removed without damaging the original paint.

How do I clean a vinyl-wrapped vehicle?

Use mild soap and water, avoiding abrasive materials that could scratch the vinyl.

Does vinyl wrapping affect my vehicle's resale value?

In most cases, it can help maintain or even increase the resale value.

What if I want to change the design of my vinyl wrap?

You can easily have a new vinyl wrap applied over the old one.
